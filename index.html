Popular destinations

Paris

France's magnetic City of Light is a perennial tourist destination, drawing visitors with its iconic attractions, like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre, and its unmistakable je ne sais quoi. Quaint cafes, trendy shopping districts and Haussmann architecture give Paris its timeless beauty.

Rome

When you visit Italy's capital city, prepare to cross a few must-see landmarks – including the Colosseum, the Trevi Fountain and the Pantheon – off of your bucket list.

London

London is a world unto itself. The eclectic neighborhoods, which are home to a blend of historical landmarks and modern-day attractions, can keep you occupied for days.

Culture

Culture of India

Dive into the world of Indian culture. It is what we like to call home.

It is love through peeled mangoes and ‘ma’s aloo parathas’; it is warmth through ‘Dhoklas’ served with coriander chutney. It is nostalgia through ‘Vada Pav’ fried in golden oil and it is the comfort in street-side ‘masala chai’ that envelopes us in its warm hug. Indian culture is love. It is tolerance. It is trust.
